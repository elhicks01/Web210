Portfolio

Blossom Beauty Co.

For my final project in WEB110, I crafted a captivating makeup enthusiast event called "Unleash Your Inner Beauty - A Makeup Masterclass." Hosted by the fictitious company, Blossom Beauty Co., the event catered to makeup lovers of all skill levels, fostering an inclusive and engaging experience. The centerpiece of the event was a mesmerizing makeup masterclass, led by renowned makeup artist, Ava Martinez. Through a responsive and aesthetically pleasing website, attendees could effortlessly register using the embedded sign-up form. The site also featured an interactive map, ensuring smooth navigation to the event venue. As a web designer, I focused on creating a seamless user experience, aligning aesthetics with functionality. The website's layout showcased the vibrant world of makeup, while intuitive design elements facilitated user engagement and event registration.

Perfectly Portioned Fitness

Perfectly Portioned Fitness: Build Your Meal Plan" is a dynamic and user-centric website that revolutionizes the way users plan their meals and snacks for the week. This innovative platform incorporates JavaScript to seamlessly collect input from users regarding their dietary preferences, goals, and lifestyle. With a simple and intuitive interface, users can effortlessly input their meal choices, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. The website intelligently processes this information and generates a personalized, well-balanced meal plan tailored to the user's specific needs. The standout feature of "Perfectly Portioned Fitness" lies in its ability to provide users with portioned meals, ensuring they maintain a balanced and nutritious diet. By offering portion control guidance, the website promotes healthy eating habits and empowers users to achieve their fitness and wellness objectives effectively. The user-friendly nature of the website extends to its convenient print and download options. Once the meal plan is generated, users have the flexibility to print or download it for easy reference throughout the week, making meal preparation and adherence to the plan a breeze. Through innovative JavaScript integration, "Perfectly Portioned Fitness" sets itself apart as a valuable tool for health-conscious individuals seeking a convenient and effective way to plan and maintain their meals. This project showcases my growing proficiency in web development, as I continue to explore JavaScript and its potential to enhance user experiences through personalized and practical solutions.
